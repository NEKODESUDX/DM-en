Mass Group Manager

Note: Due to Discord's specifications, there is a rate limit for group creation and member addition (night time is recommended for addition).

You cannot create and add members to groups simultaneously. Please complete group creation before adding members.

The entered content is not saved. Please copy it before closing or reloading the page.

Only up to 10 people can be added to a single group. Do not enter group IDs that have used up their 10 slots.

Do not press the buttons repeatedly or press other buttons during the process.

Please keep this tab open. If the browser is closed during the process, the group addition will restart, sending duplicate requests and causing bugs.

If you accidentally close the browser, manually remove the already added groups from the ID input field.
